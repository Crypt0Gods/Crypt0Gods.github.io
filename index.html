About Us

Crypto Gods and Monster was born due to Crypto Gods and Monsters was born from the idea of two brothers in an afternoon of history classes, in which they gave free rein to their imagination, something that began as a simple idea, a thought, is today an NFTs project with a lot of work , love and dedication behind it, now gods and monsters from all over the world will find each other in this new project.

We wanted to give the project a different look, a retro look but at the same time fun, it is a pixel art, but a very detailed one, each god and each monster is made impeccably and with a lot of work and dedication, as the collection progresses, you will be able to see how gods from all over the planet come to Crypto Gods and Monsters and at the same time their variants with different rarities, powers, costumes and much more!
